About me

I Am Not Creative

I'm currently a student at the Coding Bootcamp at UCLA, learning Web development in order to become a proficient front and backend developer. Prior to that I was a submarine Sonar Technician and Instructor in the U.S. Navy, where I was responsible for increasing maintaining a training lab valued at over $10 million USD.

I also have years of experience in traditional office environments and possess practical knowledge of networking, Windows, and Linux operating systems. I'm also cleared for Top Secret information and granted access to Sensitive Compartmented Information based on Single Scope Background Investigation.
